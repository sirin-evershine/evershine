Our History

At Evershine Paints, our story is one of passion and expertise passed down through generations. It all began decades ago with our grandfather, whose skilled hands and attention to detail earned him a reputation for excellence in the painting industry. He passed on his craft to our father, who expanded the business with a commitment to quality and innovation. Today, we proudly carry forward this legacy, blending traditional techniques with modern solutions to transform spaces across Lebanon.

With expertise that goes way back, we’ve mastered the art of painting both interior and exterior surfaces, as well as wall preparation and moisture control solutions. We really know our craft. Our commitment to sustainability and locally sourced, eco-friendly products ensures that every project we undertake is not just beautiful but also responsible.

We’re honored to continue this family tradition, turning every wall into a canvas and every space into a masterpiece.
